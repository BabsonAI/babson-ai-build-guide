# Submitting for Production

The full submission process is being finalized with the AI team. For now the path is simple: pass the three checks, then reach out.

## 1. Pass the three checks

Run all three and clear their hard gates — point your AI tool at each one and your project, or run through them yourself:

- **[Production Compliance Check](../checks/production-compliance-check.md)** — is it hostable? (No secrets, synthetic data, in a repo.)
- **[AI Patterns Review](../checks/ai-patterns-review.md)** — are the AI parts sound, including the required evals (a functional test suite + golden prompt tests)?
- **[Submission Readiness Check](../checks/submission-readiness-check.md)** — are you actually ready? (Prior checks passed, a real README, demo runs on fake data.)

## 2. Contact the AI team

Once they pass, reach out to the Babson AI team to start hosting. Bring:

- the **repo link**,
- the **durable owner / sponsor** for anything campus-facing (see [Lifecycle & Ownership](./lifecycle-and-ownership.md)),
- any **flags** the checks surfaced (awareness items, real-data or extra-tool asks), collected in one place so they travel with the submission.
